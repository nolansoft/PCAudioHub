What is PCAudioHub Server?

PCAudioHub Server is the desktop component that allows your Android phone to act as a wireless or USB speaker for your PC.

You must install and run this software on your computer before your phone can receive audio.

How to Use

1. Install and run PCAudioHub Server on your PC.

2. Install PCAudioHub on your Android device from Google Play.

3. Make sure your phone and PC are connected to the same WiFi (or USB).

4. Open the app on your phone and it will automatically detect your PC.
